class kernels.PeriodicKernel(h, w, p)¶

Bases: object

Represents a periodic kernel function, of the form:

$$k(x_1, x_2) = h^2exp(-

rac{2sin^2( rac{x_1-x_2}{2p})}{w^2})$$

Rasmussen, C. E., & Williams, C. K. I. (2006). Gaussian processes
for machine learning. MIT Press.
